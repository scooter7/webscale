Subject: Get to know Greenville. 

ECU is proud to call Greenville, NC home. Greenville is both a thriving city and a true college town. Located in the heart of eastern North Carolina, Greenville is a student-supportive community widely recognized as a cultural, educational, economic and medical hub. 

We hope you'll be able to visit us for a campus tour or other event soon and explore Greenville in person. In the meantime, learn more about Greenville here.
