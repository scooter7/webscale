Subject: Explore East Carolina University 

Each year, thousands of hard-working high school students like you choose East Carolina University as their destination for college. Here are just a few reasons why: 

• ECU is home to an incredibly wide range of degree programs. In fact, ECU is the only university in North Carolina with a college of engineering and schools of medicine and dental medicine at the same institution. 
• ECU is home to the largest college of business, produces the largest number of education professionals, and trains the most health care graduates of any four-year institution in North Carolina. 
• At ECU, you can receive an excellent education at an affordable price. Through scholarships, financial aid, and programs like the Honors College, our students can concentrate on their education -not how they are financing it.
